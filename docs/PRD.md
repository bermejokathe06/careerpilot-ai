# 1. Product Vision

CareerPilot AI is an AI-powered Career Copilot designed to help students, graduates, career changers, and professionals discover the best internships and job opportunities faster.

Instead of spending hours searching, comparing and tailoring applications manually, CareerPilot AI automatically analyzes job opportunities, compares them with the user's profile, recommends the best opportunities, optimizes the resume, generates personalized mini cover letters, and helps users make smarter career decisions.
